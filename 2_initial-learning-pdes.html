Initial Attempts at SINDy¶

Sparse Identification of Nonlinear Dynamics (SINDy) is an approach for learning differential equations from time-evolution data. In short, one developes a differential equation where: $f^\prime(x) = \eta(x)$ where $\eta$ is learned from the data.
